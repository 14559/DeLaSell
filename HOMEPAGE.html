Vision Statement:
To create a dynamic, student-driven online marketplace that fosters entrepreneurship, innovation, and seamless digital commerce within the Lasallian community, empowering young entrepreneurs to reach wider audiences.

Mission Statement:
Our mission is to enhance student entrepreneurship by providing a centralized space for buying and selling products, streamlining transactions, and promoting responsible business practices. Through technology, we aim to support local businesses, facilitate meaningful connections, and contribute to the digital economic growth of the University of St. La Salle community.

Welcome to De La Sell, where shopping is made effortless! This user-friendly website provides an overview of the school, featuring designated areas to help you easily locate each shop. Say goodbye to the confusion of finding Lasallian merchandise, because here, we uphold our motto: A marketplace made by Lasallians, for Lasallians.
